PCB Design & Manufacturing

Precision PCB design and manufacturing support for reliable electronic products

Brief

End-to-End PCB Engineering Solutions

VHASS provides professional PCB design and manufacturing services for businesses developing high-quality electronic products. We deliver reliable, manufacturable, and performance-optimized PCB solutions tailored for industrial, commercial, and premium applications.

From schematic design and PCB layout to fabrication support and production readiness, we manage the complete PCB development lifecycle with a strong focus on signal integrity, EMI/EMC compliance, thermal performance, and long-term reliability.

Our PCB Design & Manufacturing Capabilities

01

Schematic Design

Accurate and well-documented schematic design for analog, digital, power, and mixed-signal circuits.

02

PCB Layout & Routing

High-quality single-layer to multi-layer PCB layouts with optimized routing, grounding, and stack-up design.

03

Signal Integrity & EMI Control

Design practices focused on signal integrity, EMI/EMC compliance, noise reduction, and stable performance.

04

Power & Thermal Design

Power distribution, thermal management, and high-current PCB design for demanding applications.

05

DFM & DFA Optimization

Design for Manufacturability (DFM) and Design for Assembly (DFA) to reduce cost and production risks.

06

Prototype & Volume Manufacturing Support

Support for PCB prototyping, fabrication coordination, assembly guidance, and volume production readiness.

07

PCB Debugging & Failure Analysis

Advanced PCB debugging, fault isolation, and root-cause analysis for performance and reliability issues.

08

Manufacturing Documentation

Complete manufacturing-ready documentation including Gerbers, BOM, pick-and-place, and test files.
